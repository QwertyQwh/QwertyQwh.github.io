    About this website

    My website is by definition mine. It serves me before any others. I have a tendency to slowly drift apart, and its main function is to glue me tight.

    But it's also for you.

    For those who like stories, I will tell mine, and hopefully get to hear yours in return.

    For those who are close to my heart, this is the place to check me out.

    For those who think all self-revelations are despicable, consider this my shameless showcase.

    Be it a story, or a post, or just random mumbling, you can count on me being myself here. I do agree with E.B.White on this matter, that a personal perspective is just one man's meat, and I take no reposibility should any of you feel poisoned.
